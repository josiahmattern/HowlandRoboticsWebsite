What is the goal of HHS Robotics?

The official HHS Robotics club saying is: STEM Learning in a Safe Environment. STEM stands for Science, Technology, Engineering and Math. Robotics club helps in the understanding of many different STEM areas with the purpose of building a functional robot for FIRST Robotics Competition and demonstration sobots for school and public events. FIRST Robotics Competition adds to the STEM experiences to include student leadership, being a team player, and functional skills to be a good competitor. All while exhibiting Gracious Professionalism to everyone involved.
